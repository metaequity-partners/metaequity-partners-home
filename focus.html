01 — Real Estate

Recession-resistant
value-add.

Our real estate investment funds target value-add commercial assets across four classes selected for their resilience through cycles. We bring relationships, knowledge, and operating discipline to every acquisition.

A / Asset Class

Self Storage

Fragmented ownership, sticky demand, low capex, strong unit economics — and a national footprint of operators we already know.

B / Asset Class

Mobile Home Parks

Constrained supply meets durable, recurring rental income. We focus on stabilization, infrastructure upgrades, and resident-first operations.

C / Asset Class

Assisted Living Facilities

Demographic tailwinds and operator-driven returns. Our healthcare network gives us an edge in sourcing and staffing.

D / Asset Class

Medical Office

Recession-resistant tenant base with long-term leases. We bring direct provider relationships to leasing and tenant-mix strategy.

PHASE 02
